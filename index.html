Our Story

Why Doing Life Today Exists

Bryce Perry was diagnosed with Parkinson's at age 40. What began as a personal journey quickly became a mission to help others navigate life with the disease.

Through podcasts, videos, and a growing community, Bryce now reaches thousands of people each month with honest conversations and practical insights about living with Parkinson's.

The goal is simple: empower people with Parkinson's to take back their lives.
