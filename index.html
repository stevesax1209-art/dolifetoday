Our Mission

What is Doing Life Today?

Doing Life Today (DLT) is more than a website — it's a movement. Founded by Bryce Perry after his own Parkinson's diagnosis, DLT was built on the belief that no one should face Parkinson's alone. What started as a single podcast conversation has grown into a thriving ecosystem of resources, community, and advocacy.

We believe in radical honesty. We talk about the hard stuff — the fear, the grief, the frustration — alongside the breakthroughs, the laughter, and the unexpected gifts that can come with a Parkinson's journey. We don't sugarcoat, and we don't catastrophize. We just do life, together.

From our award-winning podcasts to the Parkinson's Community Network (PCN) App, from advocacy bootcamps to a curated music therapy platform, everything at DLT is designed to meet you where you are and walk alongside you on this journey.

Whether you were diagnosed last week or have been living with Parkinson's for decades, whether you're a person with Parkinson's, a care partner, or a healthcare professional — there's a place for you here.

About Our Founder

Meet Bryce Perry

Bryce Perry was diagnosed with Parkinson's disease and responded by doing what he'd always done — he started talking about it. An advocate, speaker, and community builder, Bryce founded Doing Life Today to create the resource he wished had existed when he was first diagnosed.

His work has touched hundreds of thousands of lives across the Parkinson's community, and he continues to speak nationally about advocacy, resilience, and the power of community.
