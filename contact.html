Quick Answers

Visit our Community page and create a free account. You'll have immediate access to forums, the resource library, and upcoming event information.
Use the contact form above and select "Podcast / Guest Submission" as the inquiry type. Tell us about yourself, your Parkinson's journey or expertise, and what you'd like to discuss on the show.
Email office@dolifetoday.com or use the contact form above and select "Technical Support." Please include your device type, app version, and a description of the issue.
We partner with healthcare organizations, nonprofits, and mission-aligned brands. Email office@dolifetoday.com or use the contact form. Please include a brief description of your organization and what kind of partnership you have in mind.
All speaking inquiries are handled through bryceperry.org. Please visit that site to learn about topics, availability, and booking information.
