Meet Bryce Perry

Bryce Perry

Founder, Doing Life Today · Speaker · Advocate · Podcast Host

Bryce Perry is a Parkinson's advocate, speaker, and community builder who turned his diagnosis at age 40 into a mission to make sure no one faces Parkinson's alone.

Through Doing Life Today, live events, speaking, and podcasting, Bryce helps people find clarity, connection, and practical encouragement for the realities of living with the disease.
