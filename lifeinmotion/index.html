Meet the hosts

Bryce Perry & Brian Campkin

Bryce Perry

Co-host · Founder of Doing Life Today · Parkinson's advocate

Bryce Perry was diagnosed with Parkinson's at age 40 and turned that experience into a platform that helps people find honest support, practical insight, and a sense of belonging.

As co-host of Life in Motion, Bryce brings the same clarity, compassion, and straight talk that define Doing Life Today and his speaking work.

Speaker Community builder Podcast host

Brian Campkin

Co-host · Author · Parkinson's advocate

Brian Campkin is a Parkinson's advocate, author, and co-host of Life in Motion – A Parkinson's Podcast, where he helps people around the world navigate life with Parkinson's through practical insight, honest conversation, and lived experience.

Following a successful IT career, Brian brought that same leadership mindset into health advocacy. He has volunteered extensively, raised more than $175,000 for cancer research, supported Parkinson Canada, and now helps listeners move from surviving to thriving.

He is also the author of From Survivor to Thriver – The Story of a Modern Day Tinman, and his family-centered outlook shapes the warmth and honesty he brings to every episode.
