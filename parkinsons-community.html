🤝 Global Parkinson's Community

A Global Community for People Living with Parkinson's

Living with Parkinson's can feel isolating, but you are not alone. The Doing Life Today community connects people from around the world who are navigating Parkinson's together.

Why Community Matters

You Are Not Alone

Parkinson's affects far more than movement. It impacts relationships, routines, emotions, and daily life. Many people find that connecting with others who understand the condition can make a tremendous difference.

A strong community can help people learn practical strategies for daily life, stay motivated with exercise and routines, understand new treatments and research, find emotional support during difficult moments, and feel less alone on the journey.

For many people, the most valuable advice comes from others who are living with Parkinson's themselves.

What You'll Find Inside The Club

The Club is the private community created through Doing Life Today — a place where people with Parkinson's and care partners connect and support one another.

💬

Community Discussions

Participate in discussions with others living with Parkinson's. Share experiences, ask questions, and get real answers from people who understand.

📅

Live Events

Join live community calls, expert Q&As, and events with Bryce Perry. Real-time connection with people who get it.

📚

Educational Resources

Access guides, programs, and curated content designed to support people navigating Parkinson's — available anytime.

Expert Speakers

Learn from healthcare professionals, researchers, and advocates through guest sessions and special community events.

🤝

Peer Support

Connect with others at a similar stage — people who share your experiences and can offer real, lived insights and encouragement.

Community Celebrations

Celebrate wins, milestones, and the strength it takes to keep going. The Club is a place of real hope alongside real honesty.

Who The Club Is For

Everyone Is Welcome

The Club welcomes people at every stage of the Parkinson's journey. It is designed for:

  • ✓People newly diagnosed with Parkinson's
  • ✓Individuals who have been living with Parkinson's for many years
  • ✓Care partners and family members
  • ✓People seeking education and peer support
  • ✓Anyone looking for connection with others who understand

No two Parkinson's journeys are the same, but shared experience can make the road easier to navigate.

About Doing Life Today

Built by Someone Who Lives It

Doing Life Today is a platform created by Parkinson's advocate Bryce Perry. Diagnosed with Parkinson's at age 40, Bryce began sharing his experiences publicly to help others better understand the disease and learn how to live well with it.

Today the Doing Life Today platform includes the Living with Parkinson's podcast, the Life in Motion podcast, a growing global community, and educational programs and resources.

The mission is simple: empower people with Parkinson's to take back their lives.
