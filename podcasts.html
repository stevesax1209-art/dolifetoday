Be on the Show

Want to Share Your Story?

We're always looking for guests — people with Parkinson's, care partners, healthcare professionals, researchers, and advocates. If you have a story worth sharing, we want to hear it.

Every guest receives full support through the process. No experience necessary — just a story and a willingness to be honest.
