What is The Club?

A Place Where You Belong

The Club is the private community created through Doing Life Today — a space where people with Parkinson's and care partners from around the world connect, share, and support one another.

Inside The Club you'll find real conversations, practical tips, encouragement, and a community that truly understands what it means to live with Parkinson's every day.

Whether you were diagnosed last week or have been living with Parkinson's for decades — there's a place for you here.
