What is PCN?

Your Parkinson's, Your Data, Your Community

The Parkinson's Community Network (PCN) App was built by people with Parkinson's, for people with Parkinson's. We knew the tools that existed weren't designed with our community in mind — so we built one that was.

PCN combines symptom tracking, medication management, AI-powered insights, and community connection into a single, accessible platform designed specifically for people living with Parkinson's.

Whether you want to spot symptom patterns, prepare for neurologist appointments, connect with peers, or just feel more in control of your health — PCN is designed to help.

App Features

Everything You Need to Thrive

Six powerful tools, built specifically for the Parkinson's community.

💊

Medication Tracking

Log medications, set reminders, track timing relative to symptoms. Never miss a dose and finally understand how your medications are working — or not working.

📊

Symptom Analysis

Track tremor, rigidity, fatigue, sleep, mood, and dozens of other symptoms over time. Visualize patterns that you and your doctor can act on.

🤖

AI-Powered Support

Our AI assistant analyzes your data to surface insights, flag concerning patterns, and suggest conversation topics for your next neurologist appointment.

🤝

Community Connection

Connect with other PCN members who match your profile — similar symptoms, similar stage, similar circumstances. Find your people inside the app.

📈

Data Visualization

Beautiful, easy-to-read charts that make your health data meaningful. Share reports directly with your healthcare team with one tap.

🏆

Gamification

Earn badges, complete challenges, and maintain streaks for consistent tracking. Because managing your health should feel empowering, not like a chore.

Getting Started

How It Works

1

Download & Sign Up

Free to download from App Store or Google Play. Create your profile in under 2 minutes.

2

Set Up Your Profile

Enter your diagnosis date, current medications, and primary symptoms. The app personalizes itself to you.

3

Start Tracking

Log your daily check-ins. It takes less than 3 minutes a day to build a powerful health record.

4

Discover Insights

After a week, patterns start emerging. After a month, you'll have data that can genuinely change your care.
