About the Show

Real Talk. Real Motion. Real Life.

Life in Motion is a Parkinson's podcast unlike any other — hosted by two people who actually live with the disease. Bryce Perry and Brian Campkin bring genuine lived experience to every conversation, mixing humor with honesty in a way that only people who truly understand can.

From navigating daily symptoms to staying active, finding community, and laughing when you have to — Life in Motion covers the real stuff that doesn't make it into doctor's appointments but matters deeply to the people living it.

If you're tired of podcasts that talk about Parkinson's from the outside, this is the show for you.

The Hosts

Meet Bryce & Brian

Both living with Parkinson's. Both determined to keep moving — and keep talking about it.

BP

Bryce Perry

Co-Host · Founder, Doing Life Today

Diagnosed with Parkinson's at age 40, Bryce Perry turned a personal health crisis into a mission to help others. He founded Doing Life Today in 2018 and has since built one of the most active Parkinson's communities online.

As co-host of Life in Motion, Bryce brings the same raw honesty and determination that defines everything he does — proving every episode that Parkinson's doesn't have to stop you from living fully.

🎙️ Podcaster 🎤 Speaker 🏃 Advocate
BC

Brian Campkin

Co-Host · Living with Parkinson's

Brian Campkin brings his own lived experience with Parkinson's to every conversation on Life in Motion. As co-host alongside Bryce, Brian's candid perspective and genuine warmth create a dynamic that listeners immediately connect with.

Together, Bryce and Brian have built a show where honesty meets humor — because sometimes laughter really is the best medicine, even when you're talking about Parkinson's.

What We Cover

Topics on Every Episode

Life in Motion explores the full reality of living with Parkinson's — from the physical to the emotional to the moments you just have to laugh about.

🏃

Exercise & Movement

Rock Steady Boxing, tai chi, cycling, walking — how to stay in motion when your body fights back.

🌅

Daily Life with PD

The real, unfiltered truth about what a day with Parkinson's actually looks like — good days and bad.

🤝

Community Stories

Listener shout-outs, real stories from the PD community, and the power of knowing you're not alone.

😄

Humor & Honesty

Sometimes the most helpful thing you can do is laugh. Bryce and Brian find the funny without dismissing the hard.

🧠

Mental Health

Anxiety, depression, identity — the emotional weight of Parkinson's that doesn't come up at appointments.

💪

Living Well

Practical strategies, mindset shifts, and daily habits that help you live fully despite the diagnosis.
