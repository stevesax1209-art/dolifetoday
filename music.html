What is DLT Music?

Music That Moves You

DLT Music is a curated music platform built specifically for the Parkinson's community. Every playlist is designed with intention — carefully selected by music therapists, neurologists, and people with Parkinson's to maximize therapeutic benefit.

Music has a unique relationship with the Parkinson's brain. It can improve gait, reduce tremor, elevate mood, and provide rhythm cues that help movement feel more natural. DLT Music puts that science to work for you.

From high-tempo movement playlists to gentle relaxation soundscapes, from uplifting mood boosters to sleep aids — DLT Music has a playlist for every part of your day.

The Science

Why Music Matters for Parkinson's

Decades of research show music's powerful therapeutic effects for people with Parkinson's.

🚶

Improved Gait

Rhythmic auditory stimulation (RAS) — using a musical beat — has been shown to improve walking speed, stride length, and cadence in people with Parkinson's.

🧠

Dopamine Release

Music activates dopamine pathways in the brain — the same pathways affected by Parkinson's. Listening to music you love can temporarily boost dopamine levels.

😊

Mood & Motivation

Music can reduce anxiety and depression, increase motivation for exercise, and improve overall emotional well-being — all critical factors in Parkinson's management.

🤲

Motor Control

Music therapy has been shown to improve fine motor control and reduce tremor severity, particularly during tasks performed to a regular beat.

😴

Better Sleep

Carefully chosen relaxation music before bed can improve sleep quality — addressing one of the most common and challenging non-motor symptoms of Parkinson's.

🗣️

Voice & Speech

Singing along to music can strengthen respiratory muscles and improve voice quality, complementing formal speech therapy for Parkinson's-related voice changes.
