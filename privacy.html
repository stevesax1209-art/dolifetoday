Privacy Policy

How Doing Life Today collects, uses, and protects your personal information.

Our Commitment to Your Privacy

dolifetoday.com respects your privacy. Any personal information submitted through this website, including contact forms or newsletter sign-ups, will only be used to respond to inquiries or provide requested updates.

Information We Collect

Information collected may include your name, email address, and message content when submitting a form. We only collect information that you voluntarily provide to us.

How We Use Your Information

This information will never be sold, shared, or distributed to third parties except when necessary to deliver requested services such as email communications.

Email communications may be managed through third-party providers such as MailerLite to help us send newsletters and respond to inquiries efficiently and securely.

Unsubscribing

You may unsubscribe from any mailing list at any time using the unsubscribe link included in emails. We honor all unsubscribe requests promptly.

Contact Us

If you have questions about this policy, you may contact us through the website contact form or by emailing office@dolifetoday.com.
