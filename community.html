Our Standards

Community Guidelines

DLT is a place where everyone belongs. We take our community culture seriously, and we ask that everyone uphold these standards.

  • 💙Be kind. Everyone here is fighting a battle. Meet people with compassion.
  • 🗣️Be honest. Share your real experience. Authenticity is what makes this community powerful.
  • ⚕️No medical advice. Share your experience, not prescriptions. Always encourage consulting a doctor.
  • 🤐Respect privacy. What's shared here stays here. Never share others' personal information.
  • 🚫No spam or self-promotion. We're here to connect, not to sell.
  • 🌍Embrace diversity. Our community spans 50+ countries. All backgrounds welcome.
